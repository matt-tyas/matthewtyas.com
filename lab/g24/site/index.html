How it works

What is Guardian 24

Guardian24 is a Lone Worker safety solution that can be used via a BlackBerry® smartphone, Windows PDA, Mobile Phone, Android smartphone or specialist Lone Worker Device.

The service is used by over 33,000 mobile and lone working staff in the UK and Ireland. Using Guardian24 these staff can log in and out of their daily tasks, leaving details of their location and expected duration.

If an activity over-runs Guardian24 will automatically call the user to verify their safety. If the user cannot be reached a nominated respondent or accredited Alarm Receiving Centre (ARC) will be notified informing them of the over-run activity.

A dedicated hot key

At this stage an agreed protocol will be followed to verify the user’s safety and location. Should the user feel that their safety has been compromised they can press a dedicated hot key on their device to summon emergency assistance.

The service will record live audio and immediately notify a nominated respondent or accredited Alarm Receiving Centre.
