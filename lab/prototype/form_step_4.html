9. Do you know how to contact your student representative?
More info

The LUU website has information about student academic representatives, plus contact details of your school rep: leedsuniversityunion.org.uk/yourreps/academicreps. Find the contact details of your course representatives on the Rep panel of the Portal. Your faculty/school will also have a staff/student forum and student representation on its committees. See your faculty/school handbook for details.

10. Have you enrolled with a medical practice?
More info

Register as soon as you can. Leeds Student Medical Practice http://www.leeds.ac.uk/lsmp is student-focused and close to the campus. help@leeds (help.leeds.ac.uk/health.html) has further medical information if you need it.
