Knowledge Safe Spaces Ambassadors

  • Leeds
  • Places: 6
  • Duration: ongoing
  • PAID

We are looking for a small team of volunteers to assist us in the expansion of the Safe Spaces campaign as coordinated by Knowledge.

You will be involved in the recruitment of city centre business into the scheme, as well as the maintenance of existing relationships. You will also have the opportunity to come up with and develop promotional activities to ensure that students are aware of the scheme and how they can stay safe in Leeds after dark.
