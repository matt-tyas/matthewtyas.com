1. Introduction

Your meeting with your personal tutor at the beginning of Semester One will help you find your feet, giving you the best possible start to university life. Your tutor will explain the tutoring system and your school’s induction process, talk to you about your choice of modules, and introduce you to the University’s student support and development opportunities.

You will meet with your personal tutor twice every semester to discuss - in confidence - your progress and achievements and also any circumstances that are affecting your ability to study.

This form is designed to help you think about your progress so you can get the most out of your meeting with your personal tutor. We have provided references to sources of further information so you can research anything you didn’t already know about.

Your answers to the “Yes/No” questions will NOT be seen by your personal tutor.

Your answers to the “free text” questions WILL be seen by your personal tutor – they will use them to help you get the most from your time at University.
