Year One, Personal Tutorial Meeting 1: Induction (Beginning of Semester One)

View agenda

University of Leeds Personal Tutoring Model: indicative agenda

Year 1, meeting 1 (within first two weeks of semester 1)

  • Discuss and confirm module choice/registration and advise as necessary;
  • If not already explained in induction week, explain PT system - how it supports students in normal course of academic personal and professional development; how referral role works to help support student through particular difficulties; how PT is student's first point-of-contact within Department/School
  • Explain that effective personal tutoring is a partnership between tutor and tutee where the tutee must also fulfil their responsibilities
  • Introduce or remind students of the range of student support and development opportunities at UoL (e.g. The Leeds for Life website/ Skills@Library/ Library training sessions / LUU / Careers Centre / Welfare Services)
