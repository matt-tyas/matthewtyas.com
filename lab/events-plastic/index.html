Individuality generates interest and recognition. So why would anyone serve drinks in blank, anonymous cups?

Printed, returnable cups from EVENT PLASTICS have an amazing amount of potential to accurately communicate lasting messages to target groups. The constant visual presence of a message on a cup whenever a person is holding or drinking from it makes this product an extremely efficient advertising method.

No matter whether you want to advertise your drink, an event or your company, we would be delighted to produce sample designs which we can continue to develop with your input.

Reusable cup systems have been used throughout the UK and mainland Europe, and with a number of caterers and bar operators including Aramark, Arena One, Eurest Sports & Food, Gastro Team Bremen, Event Bar Catering, Creative Bars, Pod Bars and many others.

The system has also been used at festivals in UK and mainland Europe including Latitude, Electric Picnic, Aerodrome, Bang Your Head, Rock Am Ring, Rock Am Park, Hurricane, Love Field, Melt!, Mera Luna, MTV Hip Hop Open and others.
